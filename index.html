About Me

I chose my major because electronics was a huge part of my childhood. I thought that they were very interesting growing up which is why I chose to study Computer Engineering. I'm a fourth year and my expected graduation date is December 2025.

As wierd as that sounds, I enjoy learning about microcontrollers. They have so many different features that can be used in many ways. The opportunities are endless!
